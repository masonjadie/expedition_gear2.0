Accessibility Statement

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

At Yaad Mon Heritage, we believe that cultural heritage should be accessible to everyone. We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities and are continually improving the user experience for everyone.

1. Our Commitment

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with wide-reaching abilities and disabilities.

2. Accessibility Features

Our website is designed with accessibility in mind, including:

  • Strong color contrast for easy readability
  • Keyboard-navigable menus and interactive elements
  • Alt-text for meaningful images and icons
  • Clear and consistent heading structures

3. Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing journey. We regularly review our site to identify and fix potential barriers. We are currently implementing:

  • Enhanced ARIA labels for complex UI components
  • Improved focus styles for better visual tracking
  • Optimization for screen reader compatibility
