Who We Are

Trail Peak Outdoors was founded by a group of passionate hikers and campers who were tired of overpriced, under-performing gear. We set out to build a store that offers honest, durable, and affordable outdoor equipment for every skill level.

From weekend campers to seasoned mountaineers, we believe everyone deserves gear they can trust when it matters most. Every product in our catalog has been tested on real trails in real conditions.

Quality First

We only stock gear that meets our rigorous field-testing standards — no compromises.

Leave No Trace

We're committed to sustainability and support eco-friendly outdoor practices.
